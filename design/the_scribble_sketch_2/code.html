Crafted for Creators

Many fountain pens are treasures to be locked away, but the Lamy Safari was born to be used. Designed by Wolfgang Fabian, it broke the mold of traditional luxury by utilizing sturdy ABS plastic—the same material used for LEGO—and prioritizing ergonomics above all else.

  • Ergonomic triangular grip prevents hand fatigue during long writing sessions.
  • Visual window allows you to monitor ink levels at a glance.
  • Iconic spring-loaded wire clip attaches securely to any pocket or journal.
  • Interchangeable nib system allows you to swap tips easily as your style evolves.
